1

AI for Health

The integration of XAI techniques into the health domain is not without its challenges. The complex nature of healthcare data, including its high dimensionality, heterogeneity, and temporal dependencies, presents unique difficulties in developing interpretable AI models. Additionally, the trade-off between interpretability and performance must be carefully balanced, as more complex models tend to be more accurate but less interpretable. Ensuring that the explanations generated by AI systems are understandable and actionable for healthcare professionals and patients alike poses another significant challenge.
